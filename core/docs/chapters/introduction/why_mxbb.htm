Why mxBB?

mxBB is a very powerful content management system/portal for phpBB. What makes mxBB a superior solution worth choosing? Here's why.

Modularity

Unlike most portals and content management systems made for phpBB, mxBB is fully modular. It runs alongside phpBB without any modification, and is able to interact with phpBB data without any consequence. Due to this modular nature, it is extremely easy to customize mxBB to your liking. Since mxBB doesn't modify phpBB in any way, shape, or form, phpBB upgrades are a painless process.

Adding new features to mxBB is made easy with the modular concept. mxBB does not require phpBB MODs to add features: completely independent modules can be downloaded and installed. mxBB modules are truly "plug and play", and are able to interact with the mxBB core without modification, which allows for a seamless experience. There are many official modules made available by the mxBB Project Team, as well as third-party modules that third-party authors have shared and made available to the public.

Customizability

mxBB is extremely customizable. With the new theming system (mxBB still uses the phpBB templating system, but extends it to add new features and functions) introduced in mxBB 2.8.0, it is possible to theme nearly every element of mxBB. With the innovative portal pages concept, new pages can easily be created at your fingertips; you need not have any knowledge of HTML or PHP to do so. Content containers, called blocks, give you easy access and allows for effortless maintenance of all your site's content. Customizing things such as size, block border, orientation on the page, and much more, are all made trivial.

Support

There is a vast array of support options available to users. We have online forums and help avenues. Our team members help as frequently as they can on our forums, helping to assure that you will get some sort of answer. Our Knowledge Base has a plethora of information, answering many "why?" and "what if ...?" questions. We also offer an IRC channel for support and discussion, located at freenode.net's servers; it's located at irc://chat.freenode.net/#mxbb.

As a project team, we are devoted to our users. Do you have some feedback about mxBB that you would like to share? We will listen! We try to be as user-oriented as possible, hopefully resulting in a product that is as user-friendly as possible.
